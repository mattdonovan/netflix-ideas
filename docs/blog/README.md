# Blog drafts

Working drafts of posts about the netflix-ideas project. Drafts live here while they're being shaped; published posts may move to a Discussions surface, an external blog, or stay here as a versioned record.

## Conventions

- One post per file, named `YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md`.
- Frontmatter at top: `title`, `status` (`draft` | `ready` | `published`), `target` (where it'll go), `started`, `updated`, and `author` (e.g. "Matt Donovan" or "Drafted by Claude (Anthropic), in collaboration with Matt Donovan").
- **AI-authored or AI-drafted posts must carry an explicit attribution callout at the top of the post body** — not just frontmatter. Format: `> 🤖 **Drafted by Claude (Anthropic) in collaboration with [@mattdonovan](https://github.com/mattdonovan).** Reviewed and edited before publication.`
- Don't put anything in this directory that isn't a draft or published post — supporting research lives in [`context/hawkins/`](../../context/hawkins/) (or the relevant subdirectory under `context/`).
